Automatically organize your saved songs into monthly playlists.

Connect your Spotify account and MonthlyTracks will sort your liked songs into monthly or quarterly playlists (like "March '26") so you can find the song you saved while on that trip or while hanging out with that friend.

How it works

Like a song

Add any song to your Liked Songs on Spotify, just like you normally would.

→

We check every 5 minutes

Our robots automatically detect new songs you've saved.

→

Sorted into a monthly playlist

A playlist is created for the current month and your song is added to it.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost?

MonthlyTracks is completely free. There are no subscriptions, no ads, and no premium tiers.

Can I trust MonthlyTracks?

Absolutely. We only store your Spotify user ID, display name, an encrypted refresh token, and sync timestamps. No listening history or any other personal data. You can review our code if you don't believe us.

I need help / I have an idea

I'd love to hear from you! Please reach out with any issues or feedback via our GitHub Issues page or send us an email at monthlytracks@clar.cx.
